## Tips for Efficient Collection

1. **Batch Research**: Research all suburbs in one city at once (same sources)
2. **Use Market Reports**: Quarterly reports often have suburb-level data
3. **Start with Premium Sources**: CoreLogic/Domain first, then verify with free sources
4. **Record Sources**: Always note where data came from for future reference
5. **Validate As You Go**: Check values immediately against expected ranges
6. **Use Spreadsheets**: Easier to copy/paste and calculate yields
